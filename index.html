Our Process

How We Work

A streamlined 4-step process designed for maximum efficiency and revenue optimization.

1

Patient Registration

We collect accurate patient demographics and perform eligibility checks with insurance providers to prevent errors from the start.

2

Charge Entry

Our certified team assigns the correct CPT/ICD codes and enters charges carefully, so that every service is captured for proper reimbursement.

3

Claim Submission

Clean claims are submitted electronically to insurance, and we track them closely to avoid delays or unnecessary denials.

4

AR & Denial Management

We post payments, follow up on outstanding claims, and handle denials with quick corrections to improve cash flow.
